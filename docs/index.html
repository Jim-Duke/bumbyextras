Welcome to the Bumby Hymnal Project website. The aim of the project is to provide high quality hymns for use by the South Bumby Church of Christ in their worship and devotionals. We produce sheet music, slides, and practice MP3s for hymns that are not otherwise available in our regular hymnals. We have produced 11 hymns in our first edition from late 2016 which we bound in simple folders to put in the pews. We are currently engaged in greatly expanding the number of hymns which we will eventually bind in a larger booklet with 100 or more hymns.
